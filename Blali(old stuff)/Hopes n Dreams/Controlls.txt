-----Game Pad-----
Left Stick: Movement/Aiming[while pressing RT]
Right Stick: Camera
Options: Menu
A: Jump/Double jump
B: Crouch, Sneak[while on ground]/Earthquake[while in med-air]
X: Shoot[while not crouching]
RB/LB: Switch items
RT: Turn on Aiming mode
LT: Use/Activate item

-----Keyboard-----
WASD: Movement
Arrow Keys: Camera
Enter: Menu
Space: Jump/Double jump
LShift: Crouch, Sneak[while on ground]/Earthquake[while in med-air]
Left Mouse Button: Shoot[while not crouching]
Scroll Wheel: Switch items
E: Turn on Aiming mode(with mouse position according to the aiming spot)
Right Mouse Button: Use/Activate item
